Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about KeralaTechConnect

How accurate is the KEAM predictor?

Our KEAM predictor uses historical data from the past 5 years and machine learning algorithms to provide predictions with over 90% accuracy. The tool is updated annually with the latest admission data.

Is registration free for students?

Yes, registration is completely free for students. You can access all basic features of the platform without any charges. Premium features are available for colleges and event organizers.

How can colleges list their events on the platform?

Colleges can register as organizational accounts and get verified. Once verified, they can post their events, achievements, and other updates directly through their dashboard.

Do you provide notifications for new events?

Yes, you can set up notifications based on your preferences. You can choose to receive alerts for specific types of events, colleges, or locations that interest you through email, SMS, or push notifications.
